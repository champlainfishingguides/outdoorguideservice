Featured Trips

Catfish Trip

Enjoy an evening with us on the water. We will provide what it takes to catch the big Lake Champlain Channel Cats.

Lake Trout Trips

We will help you wrangle in the fiesty lakers of Champlain. We slay the lakers of Champlain both summer and winter.

Champlain Ice

Enjoy a day on the ice with us! We fish for giant Northern Pike, lake trout slobs, buckets of perch, or crappies.

Fall Salmon Run

The famous Salmon River Run of New York State, get in on the action and catch some of the biggest fish of New York!
